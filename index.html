What is the goal of this test?

The goal of this test is to demonstrate how we can make a favicon icon that changes color depending on the users theme to try not blend into the tab bar.
NOTE: the favicon may not update until you refresh the page if you are switching themes.

How do I switch themes?
On windows this means going into your windows settings, and changing from light to dark. For other os's, you will have to look this up.
